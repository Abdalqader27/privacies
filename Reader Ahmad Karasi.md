Privacy Policy for القارئ أحمد كراسي (Reader Ahmad Karasi)
Effective Date: March 01, 2026

Welcome to القارئ أحمد كراسي. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
A. Personal Information
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) like your name, email address, or phone number. The app is designed to be used without creating an account.

B. Usage Data & Preferences
We may store some data locally on your device to improve your experience, such as:

Last Reading Position: To allow you to resume from where you left off.
Bookmarks & Favorites: Your saved Surahs and verses.
App Settings: Such as font size, theme (dark/light), and audio preferences.
C. Audio Downloads
When you download audio files for offline use, they are stored locally on your device. We do not track what you download.

2. How We Use Your Information
The data mentioned above is used solely for:

App Functionality: Providing the core features of the app (reading and listening).
User Experience: Remembering your preferences.
3. Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertisements or third-party trackers in the app.

4. Data Security
Since the data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security. We use standard Flutter storage mechanisms to save your preferences.

5. Your Rights
You have full control over your data:

Deletion: You can clear your bookmarks and history anytime within the app's settings, or by clearing the app's cache/data in your device settings.
Accessibility: Your data never leaves your device.
6. Children's Privacy
Our app is safe for all ages, including children. We do not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy if we add new features. Any changes will be posted within the app.

8. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
