A. Personal Information
When you create an account, we may collect:

Name/Username: Used to identify you in the app.
Phone Number: Used for authentication and account security.
Profile Image: (Optional) To personalize your experience.

We collect information about your interactions with the app, such as:

Loyalty Points & XP: Data related to your gaming sessions and rewards.

We use your data for the following purposes:

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion
You have the right to access, update, or delete your information.

In-App Deletion: You can delete your account permanently via the Profile Settings screen. This will remove all your personal data, session history, and rewards from our servers.
Request via Email: You can also contact us at 
support@alsaha.com

Our app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
